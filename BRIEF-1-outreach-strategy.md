## The offer ladder
1. **$500/mo SaaS — "Vyrrah Recaller"** (the tool). Free-week, no-card trial. The front door + proof engine.
2. **Audit** — diagnoses growth leaks; bridges tool → retainer.
3. **One-time / $5,000-a-month retainer** — done-for-you growth (delivery team in place).

The thesis: the tool delivers visible ROI cheaply → that proof makes the high-ticket
retainer an easy, warm upsell. Tool = trojan horse; retainer = the business.

## Growth targets (aggressive, delivery capacity is solved — workforce ready)

- **Jun 2027:** ~$950K–$1M MRR.

- Real constraints now: **sales velocity** and **onboarding/training the workforce onto
  a consistent delivery playbook** (not delivery capacity, which is handled).

## Channel strategy — the ROAS rule (important)
Funnels invert by channel because the scarce resource differs:
- **Paid ads / funnels → lead with the TOOL (Path 1).** Free-week is a self-liquidating
  front end: cheap to convert, monetizes immediately + recurringly, lowers effective CAC,
  and the dashboard becomes the highest-converting retainer upsell. Lower variance.
- **Cold calls / outbound → go DIRECT to audit→retainer (Path 2).** A rep-hour costs the
  same either way, so aim it at the 10x-LTV outcome. The **audit** is the cold foot-in-door.
- **In BOTH, the tool's free week is the proof/risk-reversal that closes the retainer.**
- Blended = ads run Path 1, cold outbound runs Path 2, tool is the proof layer everywhere.

## Compliance gates (must clear before scaled selling)
- **A2P 10DLC registration** — without it US carriers filter/block SMS. THE gate.
- **TCPA** — inbound text-backs OK (caller initiated); any uploaded-list outreach needs
  consent. Opt-out ("Reply STOP") is in the first text.
- **BAAs** (Twilio/OpenAI/Supabase/SendGrid) before medical clients (HIPAA).
